Module containing the types of numbers used in the package, and also their according formats.

Scats.Internal.Prec.RF — Constant

Format of real values. Default values:

  • for RT = Float64: "% .15E";
  • for RT = Float32: "% .6E";
  • for RT = Float16: "% .3E".
